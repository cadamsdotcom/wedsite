Our Story

June 17, 2023

How we Met

It began one morning in Bronte Beach, Sydney. The plan was coffee, a "vibe check". We got our coffees - latte for Chris and almond flat white for Leah - and started down the Coastal Trail, at Tamarama, we found a spot to sit facing the ocean. We don’t recall everything we talked about - but we do remember something felt right. In fact the conversation was so good we high fived to celebrate. It was a happy beginning. Enjoy some of these precious moments we captured xx

Those big 3 words

Our first weekend away was to Hyams Beach, Jervis Bay - right near where we’re getting married! It was a lovely weekend spent lazing about our Airbnb, running on the beach, and comparing our tastes in music. We still have the playlist! The culmination of that weekend was the first time Leah said to Chris -
“I LOVE YOU”

Information

Wedding Venue

The Cove Jervis Bay is nestled within the serene Booderee National Park, offering a tranquil escape just 20 minutes off the main road from the nearest town, Vincentia.

Getting Here

A 3 hour drive from Sydney, and 9 hours from Melbourne. Off the main road - continue along Ellmoos Road for 13 km; please note this is an unsealed dirt road . At each intersection, bear left to stay on the correct path leading to The Cove Jervis Bay. 

Accommodation

Accommodation is available on-site, for a cost, which we are subsidising for our guests.
Accommodation for the entire weekend will range from $200 to $350 per person. This covers your stay for up to 3 nights.
We encourage you to RSVP with your preference to ensure you secure a space on-site.

Parking

Limited parking will be available on-site and we encourage those who are staying to car-pool where possible. 
The road into the venue, is a little bumpy, so please take care driving in.
